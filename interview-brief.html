Give every agent clear title to act.

Actual Mandate

Translate emerging AI capability into trustworthy, intuitive mortgage-workflow experiences that improve speed and service while keeping evidence, human authority, exception handling and recovery visible.

Company Moment

ServiceLink publicly positions EXOS as an end-to-end technology platform for title, closing, valuation, flood and default services. Its 2026 messaging emphasizes borrower speed, transparency, flexibility and continued human guidance. Agentic workflows will increase the need for consistent behavior across channels and decision boundaries.

Candidate Thesis

An AI action should not “close” until the product establishes clear title to act: the user intent is understood, the material evidence is visible, the agent's authority is explicit, exceptions are detected, recovery is designed and a usable decision receipt remains.

Central Operating Tensions

TensionDesign implication
Autonomous speed vs. human accountabilityDesign bounded action states, explicit approval points and evidence-rich handoffs.
Customized lender workflows vs. consistent agent behaviorCreate reusable behavior patterns with configurable policy, channel and exception layers.
Multichannel convenience vs. one coherent recordPreserve intent, promises, evidence and escalation context across workflow UI, chat, SMS and voice.
Personalization vs. privacy, compliance and fairnessMake context use visible, proportional and reviewable; distinguish support from advice or consequential judgment.

Decision Architecture

The role should make it easier to decide: when the agent may act; when it may only recommend; which evidence must be shown; what qualifies as an exception; who retains final authority; what the recovery path is; how behavior is evaluated; and which patterns are ready for reuse.

Stakeholder Topology

GroupWhat the design must make easier
Borrowers and end usersUnderstand status, evidence, choices, commitments, handoffs and how to recover when the workflow is wrong.
Operations and service teamsSee exception context, ownership, urgency and the smallest safe intervention without reconstructing the case.
Product, design, engineering and data scienceAgree on behavior, evaluation cases, reusable patterns, instrumentation and release boundaries.
Lender, legal, compliance and risk stakeholdersConfigure policy without hiding it; inspect consequential behavior and preserve accountable human authority.

Change Burden

Teams will need to design and review agent behavior as a cross-functional product surface, not as prompt text owned by one function. Operators will need a consistent intervention model, and product teams will need to treat exceptions, recovery and decision receipts as first-class experience requirements.

Cost of leaving the tension unresolved

Faster automation can still produce slower exception work when authority, evidence and recovery are unclear. The likely consequence is not only user friction; it is repeated reconstruction, inconsistent handoffs, weak adoption and lost learning about which behavior is actually safe to reuse.

Clear Title to Act

ControlDesign questionEvidence in the interface
IntentWhat outcome is the user actually pursuing?Clarification, transaction state, urgency, missing context and confirmation.
EvidenceWhat facts support the action?Source, freshness, material conflicts, confidence and traceable rationale.
AuthorityWhat may the agent decide, recommend or never do?Visible action posture, approval gate, reversible scope and named human owner.
EncumbranceWhat condition changes the rules?Identity ambiguity, document conflict, regulated change, distress, dispute or unusual risk.
RecoveryHow does the workflow return to a good state?Clarification, alternatives, warm transfer, reversal, preserved context and resume point.
ReceiptWhat must remain understandable after the action?Action, evidence, authority, exception, human decision, next step and learning signal.

Illustrative Scenario Postures

Appraisal scheduling - Clear to act within bounded authority.
The agent can coordinate calendars, confirm preferences and notify participants when identity, availability and escalation rules are explicit.
Closing disclosure - Controlled assist.
The agent can reconcile data, prepare a draft and explain revisions; human release authority remains visible when the commitment changes.
Title exception - Human review before action.
The agent may assemble evidence and recommend the next step, but legal or ownership ambiguity remains an encumbrance.
Borrower inquiry - Clarify or warm-transfer.
The design should distinguish status and education from advice, dispute or hardship, while preserving the conversation context.
